Academic Research Projects

Social Competition Effect on True/False Recognition

Supervisor: Prof. Aditi Jublie

Explored how competitive group settings influence true and false memory recognition. Found that competition reduces overall accuracy but also lowers false memories by enhancing detail-focused recall.

CRISPR-Evasive Lytic LE1 Phage

Supervisor: Prof. Vikas Jain

Modified the LE1 phage to target Leptospira. Incorporated anti-CRISPR genes (AcrIIA4, AcrIF7) to inhibit bacterial CRISPR-Cas systems and defense islands, increasing bacterial lysis.

HIV-1 Cell Line Impact Analysis

Supervisor: Prof. Nagarjun Vijay

Investigated the impact of altered CD3G expression in T lymphocytes on immune function and viral progression using single-cell RNA-seq (Seurat).

Sneezing Patterns in Free-Ranging Dogs

Supervisor: Prof. Vinita Gowda

Behavioral study analyzing sneezing frequency across social and solitary settings in dogs using structured observations and ethogram-based coding.
